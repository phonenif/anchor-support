Privacy Policy

Anchor · Energy

最後更新：2026 年 3 月 28 日

Anchor（「本應用程式」）非常重視您的隱私。本隱私政策說明我們如何收集、使用和保護您的個人資料。

資料收集

本應用程式透過 Apple HealthKit 收集以下健康數據（需您明確授權）：

  • 心率變異度（HRV）
  • 靜息心率
  • 睡眠數據

本應用程式也會收集位置數據以提供地點效能分析功能。

資料儲存

所有個人數據均儲存在您的設備本地，不會傳送到任何外部伺服器。我們不使用雲端儲存或第三方分析服務。

資料使用

您的健康和位置數據僅用於在應用程式內提供精力追蹤和洞察功能。我們不會將您的數據用於廣告、行銷或任何其他目的。

資料分享

我們不會出售、分享或以任何方式將您的個人數據轉移給第三方。

HealthKit 整合

Anchor 使用 Apple HealthKit 來讀取健康數據。根據 Apple 的規定：

  • HealthKit 數據不會用於廣告或資料探勘
  • HealthKit 數據不會出售給第三方
  • HealthKit 數據不會在未經用戶同意的情況下被分享

數據刪除

您可以隨時刪除應用程式來清除所有本地數據。您也可以在應用程式設定中匯出您的數據為 CSV 檔案。

兒童隱私

本應用程式不針對 13 歲以下的兒童，我們不會故意收集兒童的個人資料。

政策變更

我們可能會不時更新本隱私政策。任何變更將在此頁面公布，並更新「最後更新」日期。

聯絡我們

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Anchor ("the App") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Data Collection

The App collects the following health data through Apple HealthKit (with your explicit permission):

  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV)
  • Resting Heart Rate
  • Sleep Data

The App also collects location data to provide location-based energy efficiency analysis.

Data Storage

All personal data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any external server. We do not use cloud storage or third-party analytics services.

Data Usage

Your health and location data is used solely to provide energy tracking and insight features within the App. We do not use your data for advertising, marketing, or any other purposes.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties in any way.

HealthKit Integration

Anchor uses Apple HealthKit to read health data. In accordance with Apple's requirements:

  • HealthKit data is not used for advertising or data mining
  • HealthKit data is not sold to third parties
  • HealthKit data is not shared without user consent

Data Deletion

You can delete the App at any time to remove all local data. You may also export your data as a CSV file from the App settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
