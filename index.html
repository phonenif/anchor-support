Anchor is your daily energy management companion. Using HRV and resting heart rate data from Apple Watch, combined with your subjective wellbeing scores, it helps you understand your body's true energy state.

常見問題

FAQ

需要 Apple Watch 才能使用嗎？

不需要。沒有 Apple Watch 也可以使用手動輸入功能記錄你的精力狀態。Apple Watch 會自動讀取 HRV 和靜息心率，提供更準確的數據。

Anchor 如何計算精力分數？

精力分數結合了 HRV（40%）、靜息心率（30%）和睡眠質量（30%）。你也可以手動調整主觀評分來比較身體數據與實際感受的差距。

我的數據安全嗎？

絕對安全。所有健康數據只儲存在你的 iPhone 上，不會傳送到任何伺服器。

Pro 版本有什麼功能？

Pro 版本即將推出，將包含無限地點追蹤、90 天歷史記錄和精力預測功能。

如何取消訂閱？

前往 iPhone 設定 → Apple ID → 訂閱項目，找到 Anchor 並點選取消。

支援哪些語言？

目前支援繁體中文和英文。

如何刪除我的數據？

在 App 設定中選擇「數據匯出」可以匯出 CSV。刪除 App 即可清除所有本地數據。

Do I need an Apple Watch?

No. You can use manual input to record your energy state without an Apple Watch. However, Apple Watch automatically reads HRV and resting heart rate for more accurate data.

How is the energy score calculated?

The energy score combines HRV (40%), resting heart rate (30%), and sleep quality (30%). You can also adjust your subjective score to compare body data with how you actually feel.

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All health data is stored only on your iPhone and is never transmitted to any server.

What does Pro include?

Pro is coming soon and will include unlimited location tracking, 90-day history, and energy prediction features.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions, find Anchor, and tap Cancel.

What languages are supported?

Traditional Chinese and English are currently supported.

How do I delete my data?

You can export your data as CSV in the app settings. Deleting the app removes all local data.
